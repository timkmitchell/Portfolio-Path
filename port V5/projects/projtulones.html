Tulones Site Redesign

Originally from Mobile, Al now residing in Atlanta, Georgia. Tulones Currency Collectors is arguably one of the most popular urban streetwear brands in the south and is now spreading globally. This is a brand that I personally wear; I'm also from that same city. Here is my take on a more up-to-date and interactive design.

Analysis: The Tulones site is function and simple but visually outdated and static. The navigation bar should be tweaked to improve functionaltiy

Goal: A more interactive design that has moving product displays, custom icon animations, more functional navbar and a new color scheme.

DISCLAIMER: This is a personal project.
