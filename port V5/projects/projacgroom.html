AC Grooming Site Redesign

AC Grooming & Boarding is a grooming service based out of Houston, Tx. They specialize in grooming dogs and cats, ranging from small to large. This service is local to my area, and this made me curious about their current site.

Analysis: This site is very outdated and visually confusing. There is not a functional navbar. Important information is omitted from the home page. The content is jumbled and hard to read.

Goal: A responsive design with a color pallete based on their building sign (Tetrad colors). A clear layout of information, including prices, location, contact info, ect. A visual that explains the product and evokes emotion.

DISCLAIMER: This is a personal project.
